On Fantasy Books

General definition

Fantasy books are defined by their use of supernatural elements and a setting defined by those elements. These elements are traditional magical in nature

Types of Fantasy

Epic Fantasy

Epic Fantasy is characterized by grand scope and scale in both the world and story. This extends to the stakes as well. This category incldues Lord of The Rings

Grimdark Fantasy

Grimdark Fantasy is chracterized by the moral darkness of the setting and the characters It generally has less magic than Epic fantasy and frequent character deaths. This cataegory includes Game of Thrones

Romantic Fantasy

Romantic Fantasy is characterized by the prevlalence of romantic tension and its relevance to the overall story. This category includes A Court of Thorns and Roses.
